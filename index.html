Our Story

Where Great Food Meets Beautiful Views

Perched high on the 3rd floor rooftop at Uttarapan Market, Dhopa Patty, Jessore Road, **La Vue Cafe & Restro** is one of Kolkata's premier rooftop culinary escapes. We bring you a perfect blend of high-quality dining, masterfully crafted beverages, and a magical open-air atmosphere that captures the soul of the city.

Whether you're visiting for an intimate date night under the stars, catching up with friends over mocktails, celebrating a birthday, or hosting a family reunion, La Vue provides a stylish, welcoming environment backed by impeccable hospitality.
